A note before the argument

This is for a particular reader: someone who reads the world through power and empire, carries a long memory of how currencies rise and fall, and finds Bitcoin unconvincing. Good. Set the technology aside. Think of it the way you think of everything of consequence – as a question of reserve power: who holds it, who can take it, and what a country reaches for when it stops trusting the assets in its own vault.
