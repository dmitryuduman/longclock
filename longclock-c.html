The Long Clock A thought exercise in five chapters

Step back from the noise

The most transformative moment in centuries is invisible to anyone watching the quarter.

Humans, machines, money, and memory are all going digital at once. Stand back far enough and an old question returns with new force: what does the world reach for when it stops trusting the assets in its own vault? Start anywhere.

Read in any order. Each chapter stands alone and can be read at a skim, an explore, or a deep level once you are inside it. The essay is the long-form prologue; the chapters are the thought exercise.
