Jensen Huang · NVIDIA · Oct 2024 – Apr 2026

One operator, one machine, traced talk by talk.

Across seven appearances – a keynote, two firesides, a live TV hit, and three long podcasts – Huang argues a single idea from every angle: the computer has stopped being a tool and become a factory that manufactures intelligence, and accelerated computing is the engine of a new industrial revolution. This is the field guide – the spine of the argument, a scorecard of his calls, the stack he keeps pointing at, and every transcript in full.

The Jensen Files

A field guide to Jensen Huang's public argument, built from seven primary-source transcripts spanning October 2024 to April 2026: the Tech Unheard fireside with Arm's Rene Haas, the GTC 2025 keynote, the Milken Institute conversation with Michael Milken, CNBC's Squawk Box, and long-form podcasts with Joe Rogan, Lex Fridman, and Dwarkesh Patel.

Quotes are verbatim. Verdicts and groupings are editorial. This is a companion to The Long Clock – a place to step back from the noise and read one operator's argument as a single, evolving thing.
